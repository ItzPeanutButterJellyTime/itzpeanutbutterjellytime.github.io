Introduction

William Felton Russell, also known as Bill Russell, was an American professional basketball player who played center from 1956-1969 for the Boston Celtics. He was the main player of the Boston dynasty and won 11 NBA Championships in his 13 year career.

He is the only player to have more championship rings than fingers, and is recognized as on e of the greatest centers of all time.
